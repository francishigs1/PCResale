    Who are we?

    We are an online trading company that specialises in the reselling of computer or PC parts. We find purpose for previously owned parts that are still of excellent quality and sell them at much more affordable prices.

    What we do?

    Our mission is to contribute to the recycling of second-hand parts. This helps to create less waste, make more availability for parts that may no longer be mainstream, and create a pay-for-value system.
